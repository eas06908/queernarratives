What do you think "queer games" are? (final reflection)

Queer games overall is a concept that has stuck with me over the semester. At first I had no idea what to expect, but queer games provide an open space for individuals to express their ideas and personal experiences for others to relate to. There are no set parameters for what a queer game has to look like or how it should function. A queer game could be a game created by an individual of any gender or identity but it helps them to share a vulnerable idea. Creating a space to be vulnerable is not the easiest experience and within queer games sticking with me as a whole, this idea of vulnerability within queer games is by far the most prominent aspect. Each project and game that I have created told a different story and allowed me to express and share different things in a form other than just text or verbal words. It is important to play these queer games to expand our experiences and step out of our comfort zones once again creating a vulnerable environment. We learn from our experiences and grow from them, and the more we step outside our comfort zone the more we are able to experience these new things. Queer games are overall a place where vulnerability exists.
